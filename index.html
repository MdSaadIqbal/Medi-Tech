About

The Smart Queue and Token Management System is designed to reduce long waiting times in government hospitals. It allows patients to book appointments online, receive a digital token, and track the current serving number in real time. This helps minimize overcrowding and improves the overall hospital experience. The system also provides dashboards for doctors and hospital staff to manage patient queues efficiently.
